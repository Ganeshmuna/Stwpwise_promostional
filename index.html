The Problem

Why Foot Health Matters

Diabetes can lead to serious foot complications that often go unnoticed until it's too late.

Loss of Sensation

Diabetic neuropathy causes gradual loss of foot sensation, making patients unable to feel pain or discomfort.

Unnoticed Changes

Pressure points and temperature changes go unnoticed, leading to tissue damage and inflammation.

Risk of Amputation

Untreated foot ulcers can lead to severe infections, potentially requiring amputation.

Expensive Solutions

Current monitoring systems are costly, lack real-time capabilities, and are often inaccessible.
